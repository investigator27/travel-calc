How to Use

Calculator, Hotels, Camera, and Install App.

Install App

Add Toolbox to your home screen or desktop.

If the button does not open a prompt, use your browser menu (⋮ or …) → Install app. Chrome or Edge works best.

Calculator

Surveillance travel, Google Maps, and billing in Trackops.

  1. Choose One-Way or Round Trip.
  2. Select your starting location.
  3. Enter the surveillance event address.
  4. Tap Open In Google Maps for directions.
  5. Enter one-way hours, minutes, and km from Google Maps.
  6. Tap Calculate Billing and enter values in Trackops.

Hotels

Search nearby hotels and send your pick to admin.

  1. Open Current Location and choose Current Location — allow access when your browser or device asks.
  2. Set check-in and check-out dates, then tap Search Hotels (~30 seconds).
  3. Choose a hotel and send the email draft to admin.

Camera

Covert video — rear camera, landscape. Clips stay inside Toolbox (not Android Gallery) until you upload or export below.

Controls

  1. 3 taps on the black area — start recording (screen stays black).
  2. 3 taps again — stop; clip saved as 00000, 00001, …
  3. Swipe up — live preview · Swipe down — hide preview.
  4. Swipe up twice (after stop) — clips library on Camera tab (view, select, send to OneDrive).

Notes

  • Android shows a camera indicator while recording — cannot be hidden.
  • Upload opens Share — choose OneDrive; not automatic.
  • Enable Haptic feedback under Personalization for tap pulses.

Camera

Covert landscape video for field work.

  1. Install Toolbox as an app (Chrome → Install).
  2. Open Camera tab — allow camera and microphone.
  3. 3 taps to record · 3 taps to stop · Upload when done for the day.
