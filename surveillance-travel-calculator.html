How to Use

Install Icon

  1. Tap the menu (☰) at the top right to open Toolbox Settings.
  2. On Android Chrome: browser menu → Add to Home screen → Install.
  3. On iPhone Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open the app from your home screen for the full experience.

Calculator

  1. Choose One-Way or Round Trip.
  2. Select your starting location.
  3. Enter the surveillance event address.
  4. Tap Open In Google Maps for directions.
  5. Enter one-way hours, minutes, and km from Google Maps.
  6. Tap Calculate Billing and enter values in Trackops.

Hotels

  1. Open Current Location and choose Current Location — allow access when your browser or device asks.
  2. Set check-in and check-out dates, then tap Search Hotels (~30 seconds).
  3. Choose a hotel and send the email draft to admin.
